A bit about me

Talking a little bit about myself, in my personality and profile I am constantly developing my front end and backend skills. with a dream to be a software engineer. creative and proactive, ready for new challenges. As children we are asked what we want to be when we grow up, most of the answers are “traditional” professions like doctors, police officers, firefighters, dancers and astronauts. However, the professional market is much broader and as I grew up I found new job possibilities, I changed my tastes, habits and preference for work. I was a teenager fascinated by computers and I discovered that I wanted to work in front of one, where I was an administrative assistant for a while, until I discovered the area of Information Technology. I started to build simple and complex systems through programming languages by myself watching videos and researching materials on the internet. In addition, I started to carry out the tests to verify that everything is working correctly.

And this is a bit about me...

Thanks for reading!

My focus is to create complex actions from both frontend and backend. Through the portfolio I can prove that I am a good programmer. And for those who have not yet developed professionally or who are curious to learn and start to become a developer, these projects can be useful to anyone interested, whether frontend or backend. Here I can demonstrate my skills, show projects and finish products or in development, instead of just talking about them. Even though I am a beginner student, I already want to join as soon as possible to learn more and share my new learnings.

The purpose of this site is to show my portifolio and a little bit about myself, my projects, my ambitions and my experiencies as developer.

My audience is to network with companys and clients interested in my work
